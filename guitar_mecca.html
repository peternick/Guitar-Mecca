This is a work-in-progress site intended to give certain tools to music enthusiasts. It just so happens that I play guitar and mandolin, so I started with those first. As of now Guitar Mecca provides easy to use tuners for both guitar and mandolin players. These tuners may be updated in the future to allow for different tuning styles and other functionalities.
